EXAMINATION RULES & GUIDELINES

BEFORE THE EXAMINATION

  • All candidates must arrive at least 15 minutes prior to the scheduled start time.
  • Entry to the examination hall without a valid admit card is strictly prohibited.
  • No candidate will be permitted to enter the examination hall after the official commencement time under any circumstances.
  • Candidates must occupy only the seat assigned to them. Unauthorized seat changes are not allowed.
  • All personal belongings, including bags, books, notes, and electronic devices, must be kept outside the examination hall or in designated areas.
  • Candidates must carefully read all instructions provided on the question paper before beginning the examination.

DURING THE EXAMINATION

  • Candidates must bring and use their own stationery. Borrowing or lending materials is strictly prohibited.
  • Only blue or black ink pens are permitted for writing answers.
  • Absolute silence must be maintained throughout the examination period.
  • The possession or use of mobile phones, smart devices, or any unauthorized electronic equipment is strictly forbidden.
  • Any form of cheating, copying, or unfair practice will result in immediate disqualification and disciplinary action.
  • Candidates must strictly follow all instructions given by the invigilators at all times.
  • Leaving the examination hall without permission from the invigilator is not allowed.

AFTER THE EXAMINATION

  • Candidates must remain seated until all answer scripts have been collected and verified by the invigilator.
  • No candidate is allowed to leave the hall without submitting their answer sheet.
  • Candidates must ensure they have collected all their personal belongings before leaving the premises.

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

  • All candidates must adhere to discipline, honesty, and integrity throughout the examination process.
  • Any violation of examination rules will result in strict disciplinary action as per institutional and examination board regulations.
  • Respect towards invigilators and fellow candidates must be maintained at all times.
  • The decision of the examination authority shall be final and binding in all matters.
